About Us

InFocus Solutions is a media consultancy agency offering editorial, translation, and content strategy services for media institutions, international organizations, and independent creators. We bring over 20 years of experience in international newsrooms, political translation, and multi-platform media production.

Bilingual Translation & Narrative Editing

  • Discourse analysis and storytelling refinement

  • Linguistic and narrative proofreading
  • Digital publishing coordination
